DESIGN & CERTIFICATION

Approved Design Organization PART-21J (EASA) DOA 21J.635 and Military Design Organization ES.PERAM21/1389/20 for the design and certification of minor changes and STCs on board airplanes and helicopters.

Compañía Española de Técnica Aeronáutica (CETA) S.A. is an aerospace engineering company with more than 60 years of experience.

Design and Systems Integration engineering services and Airworthiness Certification Engineering (EASA and DGAM) for all types of operators, manufacturers (OEMs), maintenance organizations (MOA/MRO), and other design organizations (DOA/MDOA).

  • Structural and Mechanical Design and Analysis (design drawings, structural analyses to demonstrate compliance with airworthiness codes).
  • Systems / Avionics / Electrical Integration (electrical schematics, material definition, etc.).
  • Certification Plan.
  • Compliance demonstration documentation.
  • Weight and Balance Analysis. Safety Analyses (System Safety Analysis) / HIRF / Lightning Protection.
  • Structural Analysis (Static, Dynamic, Fatigue / Fracture).
  • Equipment Qualification Reports (environmental, functional E-TSO/TSO).
  • Electrical Load Analysis.
  • Test Procedures and Plans (Ground and Flight).
  • Aircraft Documentation / Supplements.

We provide aircraft configuration analysis, evaluation of avionics or mission systems in the market, considering regulatory, airworthiness approval, aircraft integration and budgetary criteria.
