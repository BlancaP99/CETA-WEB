DESIGN & CERTIFICATION

Approved Design Organization PART-21J (EASA) DOA 21J.635 and Military Design Organization ES.PERAM21/1389/20 for the design and certification of technical upgrades and STCs on board airplanes and helicopters.

Design and systems integration engineering services and airworthiness certification engineering (EASA and DGAM) for all types of operators, manufacturers (OEMs), maintenance organizations (MOA/MRO), and other design organizations (DOA/MDOA).

  • Mechanical and structural design in compliance with airworthiness standards.
  • Avionics, electronics and systems upgrade and integration.
  • Aviation certification planning.
  • Safety analyses for weight and centering, system safety, HIRF and lightning protection.
  • Structural analysis (static, dynamic, fatigue, fracture).
  • Equipment qualification reports (environmental, functional E-TSO/TSO).
  • Electrical load analysis.
  • Testing procedure development (ground and flight).
  • Aircraft documentation, supplements.

We provide aircraft configuration analysis and avionics and mission systems evaluation, considering regulatory, airworthiness approval, aircraft integration and budgetary criteria.
